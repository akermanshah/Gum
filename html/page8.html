What type of gum suits you?



The gum suits you is Fruity Trident layers.



One of our personal favorites, trident layers is well designed, healthy, and incredibly flavorful. Although it may not last as long as some other flavors, the gum includes a sugar alchohol known as xylitol in its ingredients. Xylitol fights bacteria to freshen your breath and decrease the risk of cavities. Our favorite flavors include apple pineapple, strawberry citrus, and cherry lime.
